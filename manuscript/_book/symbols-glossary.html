Symbols Glossary

This reference documents all mathematical and physics symbols used throughout this book.

Superscripts and Subscripts

Symbol Meaning Example
\(^+\) Positive charge \(e^+\) (positron)
\(^-\) Negative charge \(e^-\) (electron)
\(^0\) Zero/neutral \(\pi^0\) (neutral pion)
\(^{1-9}\) Numeric power \(10^6\) (million)
\(_{0-9}\) Numeric subscript \(H_2O\) (water)
\(_e\) Electron subscript \(\nu_e\) (electron neutrino)
\(_n\) Neutron subscript \(m_n\) (neutron mass)
\(_p\) Proton subscript \(m_p\) (proton mass)
